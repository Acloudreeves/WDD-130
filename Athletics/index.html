The Most Popular Shoe

To no surprise the most popular shoe is the shoe of the most popular player, and the widely agreed best basketball shooter ever to play Basketball. Steph Curry's newest shoe is extremely popular and sits at a price of $160. The Curry 9's are built to be extremely light weight, the shoe itself has no rubber at all for this purpose, this shoe is meant to match even the fastest players that basketball has to offer.

The Most Stylish Shoe

Kyrie Irving plays for the Brooklyn Nets, and as already one of the most well known players he has continued to stand out with his signiature shoe line. Kyrie wanted his shoes to to stand out, and with this specific colorway of his newest shoe, the Kyrie Infinity, he does that. This colorway which released in February 2022 is meant to show Kyries love for basketball, with the love theme of valentines day and cupids arrow piercing a basketball on the left tongue. This shoe matches the hype of its player.
