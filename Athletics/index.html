The Most Popular Shoe

To no surprise the most popular shoe is the shoe of the most popular player, and the widely agreed best basketball shooter ever to play the sport. Steph Curry's newest shoe is extremely popular at a price of $160. The Curry 9's are built to be extremely light weight, the shoe itself has no rubber at all for this purpose, this shoe is meant to match even the fastest players that basketball has to offer.

The Most Stylish Shoe

Kyrie Irving plays for the Brooklyn Nets, and as already one of the most well known players he has continued to stand out with his signiature shoe line. Kyrie wanted his shoes to shout him, to stand out, and with this specific colorway of his newest shoe, the Kyrie Infinity, he definitely does that. This colorway is meant to show Kyries love for basketball with a pink shoe and cupids arrow piercing a basketball on the left tongue. This shoe really does break the trend.

These 3 shoes only scrape the surface of the best basketball shoes, there are 18 NBA players who have their own signiature shoe lines, all of which contain professional grade products. Each major company involved in sports and athletics also has a pair or pairs of high calibur basketball shoes.
