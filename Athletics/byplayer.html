KD 15's

This is the newest basketball shoe to release to date, with a release date of 6/25/2022, Kevin Durants KD 15s are expected to be another upgrade to his already outstanding shoe line. Providing excellent grip, traction and bounce they're perfect for supporting the energy of the 6'10 superstar. Sitting at a release price of $150 this is average for such a good shoe. As Kevin Durant is thin at a height of 6'10 these shoes do run a bit thin on the width. Supporting better people with thinner feet which is important to note.

Harden Volume 6's

The best left handed player in the NBA is James Harden. Made with Adidas, Hardens latest shoe has been a hit, resting at a nice slightly below average price of $140. The Harden Volume 6s are meant to give you a locked down, perfectly fitted feel when you put them on, making them perfect for players constantly on the move.

Curry Flow 9's

The star of the Golden State Warriors signiature shoe has always been popular. Especially after winning the NBA championship only recently. Known as the best Basketball shooter in the league Steph Curry is constantly looking for open spots to make his shot, the Curry 9s are meant to match this energy. They're built to give him the boost he needs to get past his opponents, to get to every open space. The Curry 9s were made with no rubber to be so light so that this shoe that will never slow him down. Shoe Price $160
